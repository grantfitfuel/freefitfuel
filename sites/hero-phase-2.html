Phase II — Build (Weeks 9–16)

Progressive overload on primary lifts; maintain Zone-2 streak; add short threshold intervals. Keep technique clean; increase load only when reps feel crisp.

Warm-up Strength Blocks Balance Conditioning Mobility Weekly Plan Disclaimer

Warm-up (6–8 min)

Strength Blocks

Chair Squat → Sit-to-Stand 3 × 8–12

Tempo 3-1-1-1; add small weight if form holds for 12.

Counter Push-ups 3 × 8–12

Use kitchen counter for more load; body straight; elbows ~45°.

Hip Hinge (load) 3 × 8–10

Hold weight at chest or sides; hinge from hips; stand tall.

Standing Row (band) 3 × 10–12

Anchor band at sternum height; squeeze shoulder blades back and down.

Balance (progress)

Tai Chi

Qi Gong

Chair Yoga

Conditioning

  • Zone-2 20–30 min weekly
  • 1× Threshold interval set: 6 × 1 min brisk / 1 min easy

Mobility

  • Calf & hip flexor stretch 2 × 20s each
  • Thoracic openers × 6

Weekly Plan

Week 9–10

Add small load to main lifts

Week 11–12

Progress band rows; steady Z2

Week 13–14

Increase threshold reps (×8)

Week 15–16

Deload if stiff; keep movement
