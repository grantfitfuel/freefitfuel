Weeks 1–4

Phase I — Foundation

Two full-body strength days, one calisthenics practice, two engine sessions, daily 10-min mobility. Simple. Effective. Fun.

Day A — Full Body Strength
Goblet Squat ▶ Floor Press ▶ DB Row ▶
  • Goblet Squat — 4×8 (2s down)
  • DB Floor Press — 4×8 (pause on floor)
  • Superset → 1-Arm DB Row — 3×10/side + Farmer Carry — 3×30–40m
  • Pallof Press — 3×12/side

Cues: full foot, ribs down; rows—elbow to back pocket, don’t twist.

Day B — Calisthenics Flow Skill
Incline Push-Up ▶ Inverted Row ▶ Hollow Hold ▶
  • Incline Push-Ups — 4×AMRAP @ RPE 7
  • Inverted Rows — 4×AMRAP @ RPE 7
  • Hollow Hold — 3×20–30s
  • Dead Bug — 3×6/side slow

Keep 2–3 reps in reserve. Lower the box/bench height as you improve.

Day C — Full Body Strength
FFESS ▶ DB OHP ▶ DB RDL ▶
  • Front-Foot Elevated Split Squat — 3×10/side
  • DB Overhead Press — 3×8
  • Superset → DB RDL — 3×10 + Band Face Pull — 3×12–15
  • Supported Knee Raises — 3×8–12

RDL: soft knees, hips back; OHP: glutes tight, no layback.

Day D — Engine Base Zone-2
  • Zone-2 — 35–45 min walk/jog/cycle @ nasal breathing

Coherent Breathing 5 min (in 5 / out 5) to finish.

Day E — Engine Intervals Speed
  • Warm-up 8–10 min easy
  • Intervals — 8×(1:00 hard / 1:30 easy)
  • Cool-down 8 min walk

Keep first 4 reps controlled; finish strong, not wrecked.

Daily Minimums Mobility
  • Band Pull-Apart — 2×15–20
  • 90/90 Hips — 2–3 min
  • Cat-Cow — 1–2 min

10 minutes total most days. Sprinkle between tasks.

Weekly Boss Fight (choose one)

  • 5k Row — Technique · No rower? Table Rows AMRAP 10' or DB Rows AMRAP 10'
  • 5k Run — Pacing guide
  • Engine AMRAP 12' — 10 Goblet Squats + 10 Incline Push-Ups + 150m Shuttles

PR? Screenshot your timer. Claim +50 XP below.

Optional — Arms & Extras (Saturday fun)

  • Concentration Curl — 3×10/side
  • DB Overhead Triceps Extension — 3×12
  • Farmer Carry — 4×40m

How to Progress (Weeks 2–4)

  • Strength: add 1–2 reps/set or +1–2 kg when all sets ≤ RPE 8.
  • Calisthenics: reduce incline height; keep tempo honest.
  • Engine: +5 min Zone-2 in Week 3; +2 intervals in Week 4 if fresh.
  • Mobility: slower reps; aim for “feels easier,” not “force range.”
