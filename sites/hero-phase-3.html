Phase 3 • Weeks 17–28

Build — Strength & Threshold

Progressive overload for strength (lower reps, heavier) + threshold endurance (comfortably hard). Log every set; use the rest timer; form links are next to each exercise.

Weekly Structure (pick days that suit)

  • Mon — Lower (Heavy): Back Squat, RDL, Split Squat + calf.
  • Tue — Threshold Run: 10–20 min warm-up, 2×10–15 min @ “comfortably hard”, 5–10 min cool.
  • Thu — Upper (Heavy): Bench Press or DB Press, Chin-up/Pull-up, Row, Overhead Press.
  • Sat — Long Z2 Run: 75–90 min conversational, relaxed form.
  • 2× Mobility: Hips & T-spine, 10–15 min sessions.

Overload = add a little weight or an extra rep/set once all sets land in the target rep range with good form.
