What counts as “Engine”?

Any aerobic work that builds your ability to produce energy for a long time: easy running, brisk walking, cycling, rowing, rucking, or swim. We use Zone 2 as the base, then sprinkle tempo or threshold in later phases. If you don’t have a rower, the Boss Fights include a Row Alternative with dumbbell or table rows.

Badges & Boss Fights

Clear weekly quests to gain XP. Attempt a Boss Fight at the end of each phase (e.g., 5k row/run or an AMRAP). Hit a PR → auto-award a badge and a small shareable card (saved locally).
