Warm-up (6–8 minutes)

Flow

  1. Neck & shoulder rolls × 8
  2. Arm swings & thoracic rotations × 8
  3. Hip circles × 8 each
  4. World’s greatest stretch × 4 each side
  5. Glute bridge × 10
  6. March in place × 30–45 s

Breathe slow; exhale on the effort. Keep ranges comfy, no bouncing.

Calisthenics (bodyweight skills)

Build joint-friendly strength and control. Start where you are; add range & reps slowly.

Foundation

  • Squat (full depth if comfy), Hip hinge, Hollow hold
  • Scapular shrugs (hang or band), Wall handstand holds
  • Assisted pull-up (band) or row

Progressions

  • Push-up → Decline → Ring push-up
  • Chin-up → Pull-up → Archer pull-up
  • Pike push-up → Wall HSPU (partial) → HSPU

Form checks

  • Shoulders packed; ribs down on pushing/pulling
  • Range with control over momentum
  • Stop 1–2 reps before form breaks

Safety first

  • Stop if you feel sharp pain, dizziness, chest pain, or numbness/tingling — seek medical advice.
  • Leave 1–2 reps “in reserve” on strength sets; avoid grinding/failing reps.
  • Recover well: sleep, hydration, balanced meals (see Nutrition).
