Low Mood

Start smaller than you think. Momentum beats motivation.

Light & routine

5–10 minutes morning daylight; steady meals & bedtime.

Move the needle

2–5 min: make bed, drink water, 10 squats, open curtains.

Connect

One message: “Thinking of you. No reply needed.”

When to get help

>2 weeks or safety concerns → speak to a clinician; see Crisis.
