1. Who we are

FreeFitFuel™ is a free, community-focused health and wellbeing resource based in the United Kingdom. We provide accessible information on exercise, nutrition, and mental wellbeing.

We do not sell products, we do not run third-party ads, and we do not sell your data.

If you have questions about this Privacy Statement, contact us at info@freefitfuel.com.

2. What information we collect

  • Voluntary information — your name and email address if you subscribe to updates or send us a message.
  • Technical information — basic, anonymised usage data (e.g. device and browser type) to keep the site running well.
  • Form submissions — the details you choose to provide when contacting us.

We do not collect sensitive health data without your explicit consent.

3. How we use your information

  • To reply to your messages or questions.
  • To send optional email updates if you have subscribed.
  • To improve site accessibility, performance, and security.
  • To prevent abuse and keep the platform safe.

We do not sell or share your personal information with advertisers or unrelated third parties.

4. Cookies and analytics

FreeFitFuel uses only essential and privacy-friendly analytics to understand general site usage.

  • No third-party advertising cookies.
  • No invasive tracking.
  • Where optional cookies are offered (e.g. to remember language or accessibility preferences), you can accept or reject them.

If we introduce any new optional cookies, we will update this page and provide a clear choice.

5. Your rights

Under UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccuracies.
  • Ask us to delete your information.
  • Withdraw consent for optional features such as newsletters.
  • Complain to the Information Commissioner’s Office (ICO).

To exercise your rights, email info@freefitfuel.com.

6. Data security

We use appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your information, including encrypted connections (HTTPS) and secure form handling. We minimise the data we hold and delete it when no longer needed.

7. Links to other websites

We may link to trusted third-party sites such as the NHS. This Privacy Statement does not cover external sites. Please review their privacy notices before providing any personal information.

8. Changes to this statement

We may update this Privacy Statement from time to time. Any changes will appear here with the “Last updated” date at the top of the page.
