Phase I — Foundation

Weeks 1–8. Build your engine (Z2), simple strength, calisthenics basics, and rock-solid recovery. Take photos for yourself (day 1, week 4, week 8) to see real progress.

Z2 Streak Strength Calisthenics Recovery

Z2 Streak (easy aerobic)

20–40 min most days at conversational pace (you can talk in full sentences). Walk/jog, cycle, row, or cross-trainer.

Strength — Full Body A / B

Form refreshers: Goblet Squat · DB RDL · 1-Arm Row · Floor Press

Workout A

  • Goblet Squat — 3×8–12
  • Dumbbell RDL — 3×8–12
  • 1-Arm DB Row — 3×10/side
  • DB Floor Press — 3×8–12
  • Core: Dead Bug — 2×10/side

Progression: add 1–2 reps first; then 1–2.5kg when you hit top reps in all sets.

Workout B

  • Reverse Lunge — 3×8/side
  • Hip Hinge (band or light RDL) — 3×12
  • Chest-Supported Row — 3×10
  • DB Overhead Press — 3×8–12
  • Core: Side Plank — 2×20–40s/side

Alternate A/B across the week, 2–3 total sessions.

Calisthenics — Skills

Push-up progression · Assisted Pull-ups · Hollow Hold
  • Push-ups: 5×(sub-max perfect reps), rest 60–90s
  • Assisted Pull-ups or Inverted Rows: 5×5–8
  • Core: Hollow Hold 4×20–30s
  • Grease-the-Groove option: 2–3 mini-sets sprinkled through the day

Mobility & Recovery

  • Daily 10 min mobility: hips (90/90), T-spine rotations, ankles
  • Breathing: Coherent 5/5 for 5–10 min in the evening
  • Sleep: regular window, dim screens late, hydrate

Weekly Template (example)

  • Mon — Strength A + 10 min mobility
  • Tue — Z2 30–40 min + Calisthenics mini
  • Wed — Strength B + 10 min mobility
  • Thu — Z2 30–40 min
  • Fri — Calisthenics skills + 10 min mobility
  • Sat — Z2 40–60 min (walk/jog)
  • Sun — Rest / Recovery walk + breathwork
