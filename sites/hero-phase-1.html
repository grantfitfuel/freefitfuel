Phase I — Foundation (Weeks 1–4)

Goal this phase: learn the movements, build Zone 2 engine, and hit a 10-minute daily mobility habit. Long run eases up to ~6–8k. Keep it fun and consistent.

Strength Engine Mobility Calisthenics Boss Fight Recovery
Weekly rhythm: 3× strength days • 2–3× engine days • daily 10-min mobility. Tick items to bank XP (stored on your device).

Strength

Day A — Lower + Push

  • Goblet Squat 4×6–10 — YouTube
  • DB Romanian Deadlift 4×8–12 — YouTube
  • DB Floor Press 4×8–12 — YouTube
  • Side Plank 3×30–45s/side — YouTube

Day B — Hinge + Pull

  • KB/DB Deadlift 5×5–8 — YouTube
  • One-Arm Row 4×8–12/side — YouTube
  • Half-Kneeling Press 3×8–12/side — YouTube
  • Carry (Suitcase) 3×40–60m — YouTube

Progress each lift by a rep or small load weekly while keeping perfect form.

Engine

Easy Engine (Z2)

2–3 sessions/wk • 20–45 min easy. Nasal breathing; can talk in full sentences.

  • Run / brisk walk / bike / row — How to find Z2 (YouTube)
  • End-of-phase long effort ≈ 6–8k run equivalent

Tempo Taster

1×/wk • 20–30 min including 2×5-min “comfortably hard”.

  • Warm-up 10 min easy → 2×5-min strong w/ 3-min easy
  • Cool-down 5–10 min

Boss Fight Preview

Optional check: 5k run/row, or Row Alternative (100 total dumbbell/table rows EMOM + 1 km brisk walk). Aim to finish feeling like you could do more.

Recovery Hygiene

  • Sleep 7–9 h (consistent times)
  • Protein 1.6–2.2 g/kg; carbs around engine days
  • Hydration: bodyweight × 30–35 ml baseline
  • Breathing 5–10 min coherent (in 5 / out 5) — YouTube
