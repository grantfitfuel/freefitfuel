Support (UK-wide & Regional)

If you need urgent help

  • Call 999 (UK) if there is immediate danger.
  • NHS 111 (England) or NHS 24 (Scotland: call 111)
  • Samaritans — 116 123 (free, 24/7)
  • Shout — text SHOUT to 85258 (24/7 textline)

If speaking is hard, Shout (text) or Samaritans’ online options can be easier.

Ongoing help (UK-wide)

  • Mind — guides & local services
  • Rethink — advice & groups
  • NHS Talking Therapies (England) — free NHS CBT, counselling & guided self-help
  • Hub of Hope — find local support anywhere
