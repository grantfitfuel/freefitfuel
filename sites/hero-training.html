Strength Engine (fun & efficient)

  • Goblet Squat (form) · DB RDL · 1-Arm Row
  • 2–3 sessions/week · 30–45 min · Linear progression (add 1–2.5kg or 1–2 reps).

Calisthenics (skill progression)

  • Push-up ladder · Pull-up progressions · Pike→HS
  • Grease-the-groove mini-sets; keep reps in good-form zone.

Mobility (move well, recover better)

  • 90/90 hips · T-spine rotation · Ankles
  • 10–15 min daily “mobility snack” beats long weekend sessions.

Recovery (where gains lock in)

  • Sleep regularity, easy Z2 days, coherent breathing 5/5, and fueling.
