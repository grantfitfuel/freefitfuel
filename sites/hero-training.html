How it works

  • Train 4–6 days/week using your phase page.
  • Z2 streaks (easy cardio) for recovery and engine.
  • Boss fights: occasional trials with PR badges.
  • Form first: every move has a “Form” YouTube link.
  • Photo journal (private) to see your arc over months.

Boss Fights (Trials)

  • 5k Row or 5k Run or Dumbbell-Row EMOM (no rower) — next trial in Phase 2.
  • AMRAP 15: Burpees + DB Thrusters + Sit-ups (Phase 2+)

Badges unlock on PRs. Trials are optional but fun.
