Deskbound Reset Flow

A short movement reset for sitting stiffness, posture fatigue, tight hips, stiff shoulders, neck tension and low-movement workdays.

5–12 minutes Desk-friendly Low intensity No equipment required

What This System Is For

Long periods of sitting can make the body feel tight, heavy and switched off. This flow breaks up stiffness with small, practical movement blocks that can be used during a workday without turning recovery into a full workout.

Useful For

  • Deskbound stiffness
  • Hip tightness after sitting
  • Neck and shoulder tension
  • Low step-count days
  • Workday movement breaks

Pairs Well With

Pause If

  • Dizziness develops
  • Sharp pain appears
  • Numbness or weakness spreads
  • Balance feels unsafe
  • Symptoms feel unusual for you

The 5-Minute Quick Reset

Use this when you do not have time for a full routine. The aim is simply to change state and reduce stiffness.

1. Stand & Breathe

45–60 seconds

  • Stand tall with feet grounded.
  • Relax the jaw and shoulders.
  • Take slow controlled breaths.

2. Shoulder Blade Reset

60 seconds

  • Roll shoulders slowly backwards.
  • Gently squeeze shoulder blades together.
  • Relax fully between reps.

3. Hip Opener March

90 seconds

  • March slowly on the spot.
  • Lift knees only as high as comfortable.
  • Let the arms swing naturally.

4. Desk or Wall Chest Opener

60 seconds

  • Place hands lightly on a desk or wall.
  • Step back and let the chest open gently.
  • Avoid forcing the shoulders.

5. Short Walk Finish

1 minute

  • Walk around the room or hallway.
  • Keep posture relaxed.
  • Return to the desk feeling reset rather than exhausted.

The 12-Minute Full Deskbound Reset

1. Neck Turns & Chin Nods

2 minutes

  • Turn the head slowly side to side.
  • Add small chin nods.
  • Stay gentle and controlled.

2. Shoulder Circles & Wall Slides

2 minutes

  • Circle shoulders forwards and backwards.
  • Use wall slides if available.
  • Keep ribs from flaring excessively.

3. Seated or Standing Spinal Wave

2 minutes

  • Round and lengthen the spine slowly.
  • Move like a wave rather than a forced stretch.
  • Keep breathing steady.

4. Hip Flexor Step-Back

2 minutes

  • Step one foot back gently.
  • Keep pelvis controlled.
  • Alternate sides smoothly.

5. Calf Pump & Ankle Rolls

2 minutes

  • Rise gently onto toes.
  • Lower slowly.
  • Circle ankles in both directions.

6. Walk & Reset

2 minutes

  • Walk at an easy pace.
  • Let the arms swing naturally.
  • Finish with three slow breaths.

Workday Use

Simple Schedule

  • Use the quick reset after long sitting blocks.
  • Use the full reset once daily if stiffness builds.
  • Pair with a short walk when possible.
  • Use before training if the body feels locked up.

Helpful Cues

  • Change position before pain builds.
  • Move little and often.
  • Do not chase aggressive stretches at work.
  • Keep movement easy enough to repeat tomorrow.
