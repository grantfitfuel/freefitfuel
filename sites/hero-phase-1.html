Phase I — Foundation (Weeks 1–4)

Goal: learn movements, build Zone-2 engine, and hit 10-min daily mobility. Long run up to ~6–8k. Keep it fun and consistent.

Strength Engine Mobility Calisthenics Boss Fight Recovery

Strength

2 alternating days per week.

Day A — Lower + Push

  • Goblet Squat 4×6-10 — YouTube
  • DB Romanian Deadlift 4×8-12 — YouTube
  • DB Floor Press 4×8-12 — YouTube
  • Side Plank 3×30-45s/side — YouTube

Day B — Hinge + Pull

  • KB/DB Deadlift 5×5-8 — YouTube
  • One-Arm Row 4×8-12/side — YouTube
  • Half-Kneeling Press 3×8-12/side — YouTube
  • Carry (Suitcase) 3×40-60m — YouTube

Engine

2–3 sessions/week.

  • Zone 2 easy effort 20–45 mins — YouTube
  • Tempo sampler: 2×5 min “comfortably hard” — YouTube

Mobility

Daily 10-min reset.

  • 90/90 Hip Flows — YouTube
  • Open Book Thoracic — YouTube
  • Ankle Calf Rocks — YouTube

Calisthenics Intro

  • Hollow Hold 3×15-30s — YouTube
  • Ring/Bar Row 3×8-12 — YouTube
  • Assisted Pull-up Practice — YouTube
  • Handstand Wall Walks — YouTube

Boss Fight Preview

Optional check: 5k run/row (or substitute dumbbell row challenge). Aim to finish with energy left.

Recovery Hygiene

  • Sleep 7–9h, consistent times
  • Protein 1.6–2.2 g/kg; carbs around engine days
  • Hydration: bodyweight × 30–35 ml baseline
  • Breathing: 5–10 min coherent breathing — YouTube
