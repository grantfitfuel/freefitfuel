Warm-up (6–8 minutes)

Flow

  1. Neck & shoulder rolls × 8
  2. Arm swings & thoracic rotations × 8
  3. Hip circles × 8 each
  4. World’s greatest stretch × 4 each side
  5. Glute bridge × 10
  6. March in place × 30–45 s

Breathe slow; exhale on the effort. Keep ranges comfy, no bouncing.

Demo (All Adults)

A 5-minute full-body warm-up suitable for most adults (approx ages 16–60).

Weekly Split

Run a 4-day week (Mon Push • Tue Pull • Thu Legs • Sat Full-Body), or a 3-day rotation by dropping one day each week. Keep 1–2 reps “in reserve”.

Safety first

  • Stop if you feel sharp pain, dizziness, chest pain, or numbness/tingling — seek medical advice.
  • Leave 1–2 reps “in reserve” on strength sets; avoid grinding/failing reps.
  • Recover well: sleep, hydration, balanced meals (see Nutrition).
