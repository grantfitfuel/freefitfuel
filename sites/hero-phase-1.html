Phase I — Foundation (Weeks 1–4)

Learn the movements, build an easy Zone 2 engine, and do a daily 10-minute mobility. Long run eases up to ~6–8k. Keep it fun and consistent.

Weekly Rhythm Strength Days Engine Mobility Calisthenics Boss Fight (Preview) Recovery Notes
Weekly rhythm: 3× strength · 2–3× engine · daily 10-min mobility. Check things off to rack XP (stored locally).

Strength Days

Day A — Lower + Push

  • Goblet Squat 4×6–10
  • DB Romanian Deadlift 4×8–12
  • DB Floor Press 4×8–12
  • Side Plank 3×30–45s/side

Progression: add a rep or a tiny bit of load weekly while keeping perfect form.

Day B — Hinge + Pull

  • KB/DB Deadlift 5×5–8
  • One-Arm Row 4×8–12/side
  • Half-Kneeling Press 3×8–12/side
  • Carry (Front/Suitcase) 3×40–60m

Engine

Easy Engine (Z2)

2–3 sessions/week • 20–45 mins easy pace. Nasal breathing, able to chat in full sentences.

  • Run / brisk walk / bike / row — How to find Z2
  • End-of-phase long effort ≈ 6–8 km run equivalent

Tempo Taster (optional)

  • 1×/week • 20–30 mins incl. 2×5-min “comfortably hard”
  • Warm-up 10 min easy → 2×5 min strong with 3 min easy recovery → cool-down 5–10 min

Daily 10: Hips + T-spine

  • 90/90 hip flows — 2–3 min
  • Open book thoracic — 2×8/side
  • Ankles (calf rocks) — 2×10

Yoga Reset (1×/wk)

Open Yoga & Meditation — try “Desk Decompress” or “Evening Unwind”.

Calisthenics — Intro Skills

  • Hollow Hold 3×15–30s
  • Ring/Bar Row 3×8–12
  • Assisted Pull-up practice
  • Handstand wall walks (comfort)

Grease-the-Groove (optional)

Pick one skill (e.g., hanging) and do 20–40s holds 2–3×/day away from fatigue.

Boss Fight • Preview (end of Phase I)

  • 5k row / run time (or equivalent)
  • Row Alternative (no rower): 5 rounds
    • DB Row 12/side (strict)
    • Table/Inverted Row 10
    • 400 m brisk walk/jog

Aim to finish feeling like you could do more. Official timer & PR badge unlock in later phases.

Recovery Hygiene

  • Sleep: 7–9h (consistent times)
  • Food: protein 1.6–2.2g/kg; carbs around engine days
  • Hydration: bodyweight(kg) × 30–35 ml baseline
  • Breathing: 5–10 min coherent (in 5 / out 5) — YouTube

Notes

Take private progress photos weekly (not for sharing) to mark your journey. Future-you will be glad.
