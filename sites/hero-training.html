Hybrid transformation

Hero Training

A 4-phase, 12-week cycle that blends strength, calisthenics, engine, and mobility. Clear sessions, weekly quests, and fun “boss fights.”

Strength Calisthenics Engine Mobility Recovery

Phase I — Foundation (Weeks 1–4)

Skill the lifts, nail positions, build easy aerobic base, daily mobility minimums.

Phase II — Build (Weeks 5–8)

Progress volume/intensity, add tempos and mixed engine pieces.

Phase III — Peak (Weeks 9–12)

Top sets, threshold work, “boss fights” show up more often.

Phase IV — Deload & Reforge

Back the volume down, keep quality high, test & reset goals.
