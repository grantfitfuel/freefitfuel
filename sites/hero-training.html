Principles at a glance

  • Strength: 2–4 working sets per lift. Add a rep or 1–2.5 kg weekly if technique stays clean (RIR ~1–2).
  • Endurance: 80–90% easy Z2, 10–20% quality (threshold/strides), 1 long session weekly.
  • Mobility: Repeat key positions you need for lifting/running — slow, controlled ranges.
  • Recovery: Sleep 7–9h; deload every 4th week; nasal breathing on easy work; fuel & hydrate.
