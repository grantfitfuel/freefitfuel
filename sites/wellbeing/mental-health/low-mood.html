Low Mood

Start smaller than you think. Momentum beats motivation.

Light & routine

Outside within 60 minutes of waking for 5–10 minutes. Keep meals and bedtime roughly consistent.

Move the needle

2–5 minutes: make the bed, wash face, drink water, open curtains, 10 bodyweight squats. Tiny wins → bigger ones.

Connect

Send one message: “Thinking of you. No reply needed.” Simple contact counts even when talking is hard.

When to get help

If low mood lasts more than two weeks or affects safety, speak to a clinician. See Crisis page for urgent options.
