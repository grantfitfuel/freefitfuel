Principles at a glance

  • Strength: 2–4 working sets per lift. Add 1–2 reps or 1–2.5 kg weekly if technique is clean.
  • Endurance: 80–90% easy Z2, 10–20% quality (threshold/strides), 1 long session weekly.
  • Mobility: Repeat key positions you’ll need for lifting/running — slow, controlled ranges.
  • Recovery: Sleep target 7–9h; de-load every 4th week; breathe through nose on easy work.
