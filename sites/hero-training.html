How it works

  • Weekly Quests: 4–6 small tasks (e.g., “Walk 45 min Z2”, “Mobility 10 min daily”).
  • Boss Fights: short tests (5k engine, strength AMRAP) — PRs give **badges + XP**.
  • Track XP: click “Claim XP” on phase pages; your total shows above.
  • Photo log (private): take a progress photo each month — for you, not for social.
