Phase I — Base (Weeks 1–8)

Goal: build **foundations** — posture, patterns, joints, easy engine. Keep it fun; finish sessions feeling better than you started.

Strength Engine Mobility

Day A — Strength + Calisthenics

  • Goblet Squat — 3×8–10, 2s down, pause, up
  • DB Row — 3×8–10 / side (bench or hip-hinge)
  • Push-up — 3×AMRAP (leave 2 reps in tank)
  • Concentration Curl — 2–3×10–12 / side
Strength Hybrid

Day B — Easy Engine (Z2)

  • Walk/Run Z2 — 25–35 min (talkable pace)
  • Alt: Jump Rope intervals 8×1 min / 1 min easy
Engine

Day C — Strength + Tempo Drip

  • DB RDL — 3×8–10
  • DB Floor Press — 3×8–10
  • Suitcase Carry — 3×30–40m / side
  • Engine: 10 min Z2 + 5 min steady tempo (RPE 6–7)
Hybrid

Daily Mobility (8–10 min)

  • Hips 90/90 + T-spine Openers (2–3 min)
  • Ankles DF & Calf eccentrics (2–3 min)
  • Evening: Box Breathing 4-4-4-4 (3–5 min)
MobilityRecovery

Trial (Optional) — 10-min AMRAP

  • 6 Burpees + 8 DB Push Press + 10 Table Rows — Repeat

Record rounds + reps. Beat it next time → **badge** (coming soon).

Hybrid

Tip: 1–2 Rest Days / week. Deload in Week 4 or 5 if body asks.
