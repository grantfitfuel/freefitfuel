Level Up: Volume & Skill

Heavier dumbbells, smarter supersets, assisted pull-ups, and longer Zone-2. Boss Fight: **5k Run/Row** (with no-row alternatives).

Z2 = conversational pace (you can speak in full sentences). Aim to keep it easy and consistent.

Strength — Heavier & Smarter Supersets

Push Superset

  • DB Floor Press — 4×8–10 @ RPE 7–8
  • 1-Arm DB Row — 4×10/side
  • Finisher: Push-ups — 2×AMRAP (leave 1–2 in tank)

Pull Superset

  • DB RDL — 4×8–10
  • Chest-Supported Row — 4×10–12
  • Grip: Farmer’s Carry 3×30–40m

Legs

  • Goblet Squat — 4×8–12 (heavier than Phase I)
  • DB Reverse Lunge — 3×8/side
  • Calf Raise — 3×12–15

Calisthenics — Assisted Pull-ups & Pistol Progressions

Upper Skill

  • Assisted Pull-ups (band/low bar) — 5×3–5
  • Ring/Inverted Rows — 4×8–10
  • Hollow Hold — 4×20–30s

Lower Skill

  • Box/Assisted Pistol — 4×5/side (slow down)
  • Hamstring Slide/Nordic (light) — 3×6–8
  • Side Plank — 3×30–45s/side

Engine — Zone-2 Progression

Target

Weeks 9–12: 35–45 min Z2 (2–3×/wk). Weeks 13–16: 40–55 min Z2 (2–3×/wk). Keep conversation pace. Use run/walk, cycle, row, or brisk hills.

Strides (optional)

After one Z2 run, do 4–6× 15–20s fast-but-relaxed strides, walk back to recover.

Mobility & Recovery — 10–15 minutes

Hips

  • 90/90 Switch 2×10 slow
  • Couch Stretch 2×45s/side

Thoracic

  • Thread the Needle 2×8/side
  • Cat–Cow 2×8 slow

Downshift

  • Coherent Breathing 5 min
  • Supported Forward Fold 2–3 min

Boss Fight — 5k Run/Row (with No-Row Alternatives)

Pick one modality

  • Run 5k — track time.
  • Row 5k — track time (Concept2 or equivalent).
  • No rower? Do DB/Body Row Trial: 250 total reps for time (e.g., 10 every 30s). Use table rows or DB rows split evenly.

Notes
