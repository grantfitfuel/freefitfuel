Anxiety (fast tools)

Your body’s alarm system is trying to protect you. These actions help it stand down.

60-second reset

2 short inhales + 1 long exhale, repeat 5 times. Then look at the furthest thing you can see to widen your visual field.

Move a little

Slow walk, wall calf stretch, or shake out your hands and arms for 20–30 seconds. Lengthen exhale as you move.

Reduce inputs

Step away from scrolling, lower volume, dim a light. Fewer inputs = easier for the nervous system to settle.

Build a buffer

Sleep window, morning light, hydration, and a simple plan for the day. Small routines reduce baseline anxiety.
