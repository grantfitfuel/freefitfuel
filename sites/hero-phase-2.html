Hero Training · Month 2

Phase II — Build

We turn the foundations into momentum: more volume, tidy technique, and a steady **Zone-2 streak** for engine. Fun, bright, and intentionally challenging.

Strength Calisthenics Engine (Z2) Mobility Boss Fight

What Phase II adds

  • Strength: 3×/week full-body with smart supersets (push/pull, hinge/squat).
  • Calisthenics: progress to eccentrics and assisted reps.
  • Engine: keep the Z2 streak (2–3 easy sessions/wk; nasal breathing, can talk in sentences).
  • Mobility: 10–15 min resets on training days (hips/ankles/shoulders).

Z2 = heart-rate zone where you could hold a convo; think easy jog/brisk walk/cycle — it quietly builds your endurance “battery.”

Full-Body A (push emphasis)

  • DB Floor Press — 4×8–10 (2s down, pause)
  • 1-Arm DB Row — 4×10/side
  • Goblet Squat — 4×8–12
  • DB RDL — 3×10
  • Plank — 3×40–60s

Tip: last 2 reps should feel challenging but clean. Add 1–2 kg when all sets hit the top rep.

Full-Body B (pull/legs)

  • DB Overhead Press — 4×6–8
  • Hip Hinge / KB Swings — 4×12–15 (light/mod)
  • Reverse Lunge — 3×8/side
  • Concentration Curl — 3×10/side
  • Side Plank — 3×30–45s/side
Superset: Press ↔ RowTempo Control

Upper Body Progressions

  • Assisted Pull-ups — 4×4–6 (band/chair)
  • Eccentric Pull-ups — 3×3–5 (5s down)
  • Elevated Push-ups — 4×8–12
  • Ring/Table Rows — 4×8–12

Lower Body & Core

  • Assisted Pistol Squat — 3×6–8/side
  • Hip Thrust — 4×12–15
  • Hollow Hold — 4×20–30s
  • Dead Bug — 3×8/side

Zone-2 Streak (2–3×/week)

  • Z2 Run/Walk — 30–45 min conversational pace
  • Z2 Cycle — 45–60 min easy spin
  • Z2 Row — 25–40 min smooth strokes

Talk test ✅ / nasal breathing ✅ / finish feeling fresh ✅.

Tempo Bite (1×/week)

  • Tempo Run — 10 min easy + 10–15 min comfortably hard + easy cool-down
  • Row Intervals — 6×2 min hard / 1 min easy

Daily Reset (10–12 min)

  • 90/90 Hips — 2×60s/side
  • T-Spine Openers — 2×8/side
  • Ankle Dorsiflexion — 2×60s/side

Post-Session Cool-down (6–8 min)

  • Box Breathing — 2–3 min (4-4-4-4)
  • Child’s Pose — 2–3 min
  • Supine Twist — 60–90s/side

Suggested Week (move sessions as needed)

  • Mon: Strength A + Mobility
  • Tue: Z2 (30–45 min)
  • Wed: Calisthenics + Core
  • Thu: Strength B + Mobility
  • Fri: Z2 (45–60 min)
  • Sat: Tempo Bite (run or row)
  • Sun: Off / Easy Walk / Stretch

Boss Fight — 5k Benchmark (pick one)

Choose your arena. Log time or reps — beat it next month for a PR badge.

  • Run 5k — steady, even pace. Pacing tips
  • Row 5k — smooth strokes at consistent split. Row form
No rower? Strength alternative:
  • Table/Inverted Rows — AMRAP 10 min (good reps)
