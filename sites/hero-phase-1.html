Weeks 1–4

Phase I — Foundation

Skill first, volume second. Two full-body strength days, one calisthenics practice, two engine pieces, daily 10-minute mobility minimums.

Back to Hero Training

Day A — Full Body Strength

  • Goblet Squat — 4×8 (2s down)
  • DB Floor Press — 4×8 (pause on floor)
  • Superset → 1-Arm DB Row — 3×10/side + Farmer Carry — 3×30–40m
  • Pallof Press — 3×12/side

Form cues: ribs down, full foot pressure; rows—shoulder blade to spine, no twisting.

Day B — Calisthenics Flow Skill

  • Incline Push-Ups — 4×AMRAP @ RPE 7
  • Inverted Rows — 4×AMRAP @ RPE 7
  • Hollow Hold — 3×20–30s
  • Dead Bug — 3×6/side slow

Keep reps in reserve; quality beats fatigue. Elevate hands as needed to keep perfect lines.

Day C — Full Body Strength

  • Front-Foot Elevated Split Squat — 3×10/side
  • DB Overhead Press — 3×8
  • Superset → DB RDL — 3×10 + Band Face Pull — 3×12–15
  • Supported Knee Raises — 3×8–12

RDL: hips back, shins near vertical, maintain neutral spine; press: ribs down, glutes on.

Day D — Engine Base Zone-2

  • Zone-2 — 35–45 min walk/jog/cycle @ nasal breathing
Cool-down — Coherent Breathing 5 min (in 5 / out 5)

Day E — Engine Intervals Speed

  • Warm-up 8–10 min easy
  • Intervals — 8×(1:00 hard / 1:30 easy)
  • Cool-down 8 min walk

Keep first 4 reps controlled; finish strong, not destroyed.

Daily Minimums Mobility

  • Band Pull-Apart — 2×15–20
  • 90/90 Hips — 2–3 min easy flow
  • Cat-Cow — 1–2 min

Aim for 10 minutes total most days. Scatter through your day if needed.

Optional — Arms & Extras (Saturday fun)

  • Concentration Curl — 3×10/side
  • DB Overhead Triceps Extension — 3×12
  • Farmer Carry — 4×40m (add grip challenge)

Weekly “Boss Fight” (choose one)

  • 5k Row — Technique · No rower? Do Table Rows AMRAP 10' or 1-Arm DB Rows AMRAP 10'.
  • 5k Run — Pacing guide
  • Engine AMRAP 12' — 10 Goblet Squats + 10 Incline Push-Ups + 150m Shuttles.

How to Progress (Weeks 2–4)

  • Strength: add 1–2 reps per set or +1–2 kg when all sets @ RPE ≤8.
  • Calisthenics: reduce incline height, keep perfect tempo.
  • Engine: +5 min Zone-2 in Week 3; +2 intervals (total 10) in Week 4 if fresh.
  • Mobility: same drills, slower reps; aim to “feel easier” rather than force range.
