Support (UK & Scotland)

If you need urgent help

  • 999 (UK) — call if you or someone else is in immediate danger
  • Samaritans — 116 123 (free, 24/7, UK & ROI)
  • Shout — text SHOUT to 85258 (24/7 crisis textline, UK)

Ongoing support

  • Mind — guides & local services
  • Rethink Mental Illness — advice & peer groups
  • NHS Talking Therapies (England) — self-referral via GP or online
  • NHS Inform Scotland — local services & helplines
  • Breathing Space (Scotland) — phone 0800 83 85 87, evenings & weekends
  • Hafal (Wales) — mental health support
  • MindWise (Northern Ireland) — services & advocacy

Forces & Emergency Responders

  • Combat Stress — veterans’ mental health
  • Veterans’ Gateway — 24/7 first point of contact
  • NHS Op COURAGE (England) — specialist NHS care for serving and veterans
  • Mind Blue Light — police, fire, ambulance, search & rescue
  • Police Care UK — support for serving & former police and families
  • The Fire Fighters Charity — recovery & wellbeing
  • TASC — The Ambulance Staff Charity
