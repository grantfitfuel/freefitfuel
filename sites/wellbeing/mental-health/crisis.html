Crisis & Urgent Help

If you feel at risk of harm, get immediate help.

Right now

If you or someone else is in immediate danger, call emergency services.

Speak to a clinician

Contact your GP or local out-of-hours service for urgent mental health advice.

Talk to someone

Reach out to a trusted person, or use a local listening/support line. Add your local services here.

Make your space safer

Reduce access to means, move to a shared or public area, and keep your phone with you.
