Support (UK-wide & Regional)

If you need urgent help

  • Call 999 (UK) if there is immediate danger.
  • NHS 111 (England) or NHS 24 (Scotland: call 111) for urgent medical advice.
  • Samaritans — call 116 123 (free, 24/7, UK & ROI) — samaritans.org
  • Shout (text support) — text SHOUT to 85258 (free, 24/7, UK) — giveusashout.org

Tip: If speaking is hard, Shout (text) or Samaritans’ online chat (when available) can be easier.

Ongoing help (UK-wide)

  • Mind — info & local services: mind.org.uk
  • Rethink Mental Illness — advice & groups: rethink.org
  • NHS Talking Therapies (England): find-a-therapy-service
  • Hub of Hope — find local mental health support: hubofhope.co.uk

England

  • Urgent Mental Health Helplines (24/7, NHS): nhs.uk/service-search/mental-health
  • NHS 111: 111.nhs.uk

Scotland

  • Breathing Space — 0800 83 85 87 (evenings & weekends): breathingspace.scot
  • NHS Inform Scotland — mental health & local services: nhsinform.scot
  • NHS 24 — 111: nhs24.scot

Wales

  • C.A.L.L. (Community Advice & Listening Line) — 0800 132 737 or text HELP to 81066: callhelpline.org.uk
  • NHS 111 Wales: 111.wales.nhs.uk

Northern Ireland

  • Lifeline — 0808 808 8000 (free, 24/7): lifelinehelpline.info
  • NI Direct — mental health services: nidirect.gov.uk

Specialist helplines

  • PAPYRUS HOPELINE247 (under 35 suicide prevention) — 0800 068 4141, text 88247: papyrus-uk.org
  • CALM (Campaign Against Living Miserably) — 0800 58 58 58 (5pm–midnight daily): thecalmzone.net

Young people & families

  • Childline — 0800 1111: childline.org.uk
  • YoungMinds (Parents Helpline): parents helpline & webchat

For veterans & forces community

  • Veterans’ Gateway (24/7): veteransgateway.org.uk
  • Combat Stress (trauma/nightmares/PTSD): combatstress.org.uk
  • NHS Op COURAGE (England): NHS Op COURAGE

Emergency responders

  • Police Care UK: policecare.org.uk
  • The Fire Fighters Charity: firefighterscharity.org.uk
  • TASC — The Ambulance Staff Charity: theasc.org.uk
