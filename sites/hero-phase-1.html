Phase I — Foundation (Weeks 1–8)

Master form, build a steady Zone-2 engine, and cement recovery habits. Click any exercise name to open YouTube form demos. Log sets/reps/kg and use the timer for rest control.

Warm-up Strength Circuit Balance Conditioning Mobility Cool-down Weekly Plan Disclaimer

Warm-up (6–8 min)

  • Neck & shoulder circles × 8
  • Arm swings, thoracic rotations × 8
  • Hip circles × 8; ankle circles × 10 each
  • Calf stretch (wall) 2 × 20s each

Strength Circuit (10–15 min)

Sit-to-Stand 2–3 × 8–12

From a chair, stand; sit slowly (3-count down). Progress by pausing 2s at bottom or holding a light weight.

Wall Push-ups 2–3 × 8–12

Body straight, elbows ~45°. Progress to kitchen-counter height when 12 is easy.

Hip Hinge 2–3 × 8–12

Push hips back, slight knee bend, long spine; stand tall. Add light weight if smooth.

March & Balance 2 × 30–45s

March in place; progress to 10–20s single-leg stands (hold support if needed).

Rest 30–60s between moves. If new or managing conditions, check with your clinician.

Balance (gentle options)

Tai Chi

Qi Gong

Chair Yoga

Conditioning

  • Zone-2 walk 10–20 min.
  • Add 1–2 brisk intervals of 2 min from Week 3.

Mobility Cool-down (3–4 min)

  • Calf stretch 2 × 20s each
  • Hip flexor stretch 2 × 20s each
  • Thoracic openers × 6

Weekly Plan

Week 1

3 sessions • 30–35 min • Learn form

Week 2

3 sessions • 35–40 min • +1 set if smooth

Week 3

3 sessions • 35–40 min • Add brisk intervals

Week 4

3 sessions • 40–45 min • Tempo focus

Week 5–8

Hold steady, add small load when easy

Important Note

This plan is general guidance and not a substitute for personalised medical advice. If unsure, consult a clinician before starting.
