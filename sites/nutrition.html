Macros 101

Protein rebuilds muscle; carbs fuel effort; fats support hormones and vitamins. Aim for protein each meal, slow carbs, and a thumb of healthy fats.

Hydration

Guide: ~30–35 ml/kg/day. Add ~500–1000 ml around workouts depending on sweat and duration. Pale straw urine = generally well hydrated.
