How This Works

The Recovery & Training Hub is connected to the wider FreeFitFuel system architecture. My Plan can recommend systems automatically based on goals, recovery needs, mobility restrictions and injury-aware selections.

Each system is designed to work either:

  • As a standalone recovery or movement routine
  • Alongside your training plan
  • As part of phased rehabilitation or rebuilding
  • As a daily reset flow for consistency and recovery
