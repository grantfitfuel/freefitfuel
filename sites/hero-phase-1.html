Hero Training

Phase I — Foundation

Weeks 1–4. Learn pristine form, build a steady **Zone-2 engine**, and lay down daily mobility. All exercises link to YouTube search for quick form refreshers.

💪 Strength 🏃 Engine 🧗 Calisthenics 🧘 Mobility 🛌 Recovery ⚔️ Boss Fights
Level 1 • Apprentice
100 XP

Day 1 — Strength (Upper)

💪 Strength🧗 Skill
  • DB Floor Press — 4×8 @ RPE 7
  • 1-Arm DB Row — 4×10/side
  • Concentration Curl — 3×12/side
  • Band Pull-Apart — 3×15

Tempo: 2s down, 1s pause if needed, smooth up. RPE 7 ≈ 3 reps in reserve.

Day 2 — Engine (Z2)

🏃 Engine🛌 Easy
  • Zone-2 Cardio — 35–45 min (walk/jog/row/bike)
  • Nasal Breathing — keep conversation pace

Keep HR where you can talk in full sentences; you should finish feeling better than you started.

Day 3 — Strength (Lower)

💪 Strength
  • Goblet Squat — 4×8
  • DB RDL — 4×10
  • Split Squat — 3×10/side
  • Calf Raise — 3×15

Day 4 — Calisthenics + Mobility

🧗 Calisthenics🧘 Mobility
  • Push-Up Progression — 4×AMRAP (leave 2 reps)
  • Bar Hang / Scap Pull-up — 4×20–30s / 6–8
  • 10-min Hip & T-spine Flow

Day 5 — Engine (Intervals)

🏃 Engine🚥 Threshold
  • 4×4 min @ hard-but-steady — 2 min easy between
  • Breathing focus — smooth exhales

Day 6 — Optional Skill / Fun

🎯 Optional
  • Light Turkish Get-Ups — 10 min easy practice
  • Farmer Carry — 5×40–60m

Keep it playful. Stop well before fatigue.

Day 7 — Recovery

🛌 Recovery
  • Coherent Breathing — 5–10 min
  • Full-body Mobility — 10–15 min

Week 2 — Notes

Add +1 set to first strength lift; Z2 +5 min; keep form crisp.

Week 3 — Notes

Progress weights 2–5% if all sets were clean; intervals become 5×3 min.

Week 4 — Deload & Test

Drop volume ~30%, keep intensity moderate. Optional: short Boss Fight (10-min AMRAP) to benchmark.
